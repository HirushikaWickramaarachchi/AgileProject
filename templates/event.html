About This Event

AI Mini Hackathon is a student-focused innovation day where participants form small teams and build AI-powered prototypes around real campus challenges. You can join with a team or register individually and get matched on the day.

The session includes guided brainstorming, technical support from mentors, mini-checkpoints, and final pitches. Whether you are curious about your first AI project or already experienced, the format is designed to help you create something meaningful.
